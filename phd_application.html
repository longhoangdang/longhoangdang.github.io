2024-2025 Intake

I am currently seeking undergraduate students (2nd, 3rd, and 4th year) for research opportunities. Selected students will have the chance to collaborate with experts in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence (ML&AI) and potentially submit their work to prestigious conferences and journals in these fields. Students interested in working with me on ML&AI projects under my supervision are encouraged to send their applications via email.

Research Topics

Below, I list some of my broad research topics which I expect prospective students will work on under my supervision. If you are interested in these topics or have any other research proposals, don't hesitate to contact me and we can discuss further 😄.

  • Generative Models
  • Vision and Language Reasoning
  • Large Vision-Language Models
  • Domain Generalization

Required Documents

When you contact me, please send me the following documents via email:

  • A resume (CV)
  • Current academic transcripts
  • Awards in Mathematics or Programming (if any)
  • Published papers and scientific reports in English (if any)
  • A globally accepted English language certificate (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL, PTE) (if any)

My list of documents may not be exhaustive and you are encouraged to include other documents you think could demonstrate your research capability. Awards in Mathematics or Programming and Published papers are important since it will make you stand out from other candidates.

Selection Criteria

  • Thirst of conquering the unknowns and willing to pursue a research career.
  • Research experience (e.g. has published papers).
  • Academic recognition: university prizes and awards. High quality honours at high school may also count.
  • Good math background (we will use a lot of statistics, optimization and linear algebra).
  • Good programming skills. An ability to scale up to large datasets is a bonus.
  • Good English. Good critical reading and writing skills are vital. Please note that a student could be interviewed without meeting this requirement
